Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on the service. PPC ads can drive traffic within days. SEO typically takes 3-6 months for significant results. Social media growth happens gradually over 2-4 months. We'll set realistic expectations during your strategy session.

What's your pricing structure?

We create custom packages based on your goals and budget. Monthly retainers typically start at $1,500/month for basic services and scale based on scope. We'll provide a detailed proposal after our initial consultation.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

We've worked with 15+ industries including e-commerce, SaaS, local services, healthcare, real estate, and more. Every industry has unique challenges, and we customize our approach accordingly.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

We offer monthly agreements (no long-term lock-in). If you're not seeing progress, we'll adjust our strategy. If you're still not satisfied, you can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Your success is our only metric.

Will I have a dedicated account manager?

Yes! You'll have a dedicated strategist who knows your business inside-out. Plus, you'll have direct access to our team via Slack, email, or phone. No ticket systems or waiting days for responses.

Can you guarantee results?

No one can guarantee specific rankings or results (and run from anyone who does). What we CAN guarantee: transparent reporting, constant optimization, and strategies based on proven data. Our 98% retention rate speaks for itself.
